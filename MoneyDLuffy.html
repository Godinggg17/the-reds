Monkey D. Luffy

The next Pirate King

Monkey D. Luffy is the founder and captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, seeking the legendary treasure to become the Pirate King and attain ultimate freedom. Born to revolutionary Monkey D. Dragon and marine hero Monkey D. Garp, Luffy was inspired by "Red-Haired" Shanks, who gave him his signature straw hat and encouraged his dream of becoming a King of Pirates.

The Journey

  • East Blue - Luffy recruits a diverse crew of pirates, including Zoro, Nami, Usopp, and Sanji, as they journey through the East Blue Sea
  • Arabasta - Luffy and his crew as they travel to the desert kingdom of Arabasta, where they help Princess Vivi overthrow the tyrannical warlord Crocodile and his Baroque Works organization, ultimately preventing a civil war.
  • Sky Island - Luffy and his crew journey to the floating island of Skypiea, where they uncover its ancient secrets, battle the ruthless Enel, and defend the island's inhabitants from his oppressive rule.
  • Water 7 - Luffy and his crew as they arrive in the city of Water 7, where they become embroiled in a conflict involving the mysterious shipwrights, the betrayal of Nico Robin, and the discovery of a dangerous conspiracy linked to the powerful World Government, culminating in a battle against the enigmatic CP9.
  • Thriller Bark - Luffy leads the Straw Hat Pirates as they face the eerie island of Thriller Bark, battling Gecko Moria and his army of zombies to rescue their crewmates, ultimately pushing himself to the limit to defeat Moria and save his friends.
  • Summit War - Luffy desperately fights to rescue his brother Ace from execution, leading to a massive battle at Marineford between the Whitebeard Pirates, the Marines, and the Shichibukai, ultimately resulting in heart-wrenching loss and setting the stage for Luffy's growth and future adventures.
  • Fish-Man Island - Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates journeying beneath the sea to the underwater kingdom of Fish-Man Island, where they battle the oppressive rule of the New Fish-Man Pirates and confront deep-rooted racial tensions between fish-men and humans, all while uncovering the island's dark history.
  • Dressrosa - The Crew ally with the gladiators, rebels, and the pirate Trafalgar Law to overthrow the tyrannical rule of Donquixote Doflamingo, uncovering his sinister plans involving the Smile factory, the mysterious underworld, and a powerful ancient weapon.
  • Whole Cake Island - The Crew infiltrate Big Mom’s territory to rescue Sanji from an arranged marriage, while navigating a perilous world of political intrigue, powerful enemies, and the dark secrets of the Charlotte family.
  • Wano Country - Luffy join forces with the samurai of Wano to overthrow the tyrannical rule of Kaido, uncovering deep political conspiracies, legendary powers, and the island’s connection to the greater world while preparing for an epic war to bring freedom to Wano.
  • Egghead - Luffy arrive at a futuristic island ruled by Dr. Vegapunk, where they uncover shocking truths about the world’s technological advancements, face off against the World Government’s forces, and discover secrets that could change the course of history.
  • Elbaph (still unfolding) - Luffy and the Straw Hat Pirates arriving at the legendary land of giants, where they confront new allies and enemies, explore the island’s rich history tied to the giants' culture, and uncover the mysteries that link Elbaph to the broader conflict against the World Government.
